Political Parties

Know your leaders before casting your vote.

TDP
N. Chandrababu Naidu
President of the Telugu Desam Party, Chandrababu Naidu served multiple terms as Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh. He is widely recognised for championing technology-led governance and large-scale infrastructure development.
Jana Sena Party
Pawan Kalyan
Founder and president of Jana Sena Party, Pawan Kalyan channels his popularity as a leading actor into advocacy for government transparency, youth political participation, and community welfare.
BJP
Daggubati Purandeswari
A senior BJP leader from Andhra Pradesh, Purandeswari has held several ministerial portfolios covering education, commerce, and public administration, building a reputation as a policy-oriented legislator.
YSRCP
Y. S. Jagan Mohan Reddy
Founder and president of the YSR Congress Party, Jagan Mohan Reddy served as Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh and is known for launching a series of direct-benefit welfare programmes for citizens.
